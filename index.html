Modern Subsistence

Food, water, clothing, and shelter are limited resources that are required for individuals to survive.

Although we have made great advances in technology, these basic needs are still not guranteed to everyone today. Healthcare and education are institutions designed to advance the mind and maintain the well being of the People. Although modern socities acknowledge the need for these systems, they come at a cost. Today this cost limits its access.

Modern subsistence is the idea that in addition to faciliting our basic needs, the main function of society is to provide education and health services to all who desire it.

Free and Open Source Solutions

By reimaging education, healthcare, finance, technology and food security we are starting to transform our communities by working towards a common goal in a new way.

PnL is working to build technologies in the same spirit as Free and Open Source Software to create solutions that work towards modern subsistence for all.

PnL EDU: The blockchain based education tool for all ages. Standardized curriculums crowdsourced and catered for each unique communities needs.

PnL Health: IOT and wearable devices for the monitoring and analysis of basic health information.

PnL Plants: Home gardens and community improved agriculture using precision agriculture.

PnL Clothing: The clothing line that gives back.

PnL Recycling: Keeping communities clean and building sustainable dwelling units.



The PnL CLI is the first step towards making crowd configured technologies accessible to the masses.
